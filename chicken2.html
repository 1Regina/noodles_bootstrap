Chicken Curry Noodles

For that spicy shiokness

Ingredients

serves 4
  1. 4 pieces chicken drumsticks, steamed till thoroughly cooked
  2. 1 packet instant curry paste
  3. 1 red onion, peeled & finely chopped
  4. 2 tbsp dried shrimps, rinsed & toasted in pan
  5. 300g baby potatoes, halved
  6. 70g fried beancurd puffs (tau pok), cut into bite size triangles
  7. 100g fish cake, sliced
  8. 160g bean sprouts, blanched in boiling water
  9. 200ml coconut milk
  10. 650ml water
  11. 250g thick bee hoon, blanched in boiling water
  12. 250g yellow noodles, blanched in boiling water

Instructions

45 minutes
Preparation Serving
Preparation
  1. Heat curry paste in pan. Add onions and dried shrimps and stir-fry till fragrant.
  2. Add water and potatoes and bring to a boil.
  3. Reduce heat to simmer for 15-20 minutes or till potatoes are tender.
  4. Stir in coconut milk and add beancurd puffs & fish cake. Simmer for another 5 minutes.
Serving
  1. To serve, split the bee hoon and noodles into 4 individual bowls.
  2. Top with chicken and beansprouts and ladle the curry mixture over.
